1. During the game, you can press the arrow keys to move the player in that direction respectively

2. Press A to drop the balloons at that location

3. Press B to taunt the enemy in the screen

4. If the enemy collides with any balloon, it will die and you win the game because there is one enemy only for now

5. If the enemy collides with player, then the player dies and you lose the game
